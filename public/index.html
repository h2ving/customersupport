Product Recall Roleplay

Background:

TechGadget Inc., a leading electronics manufacturer, has recently discovered a safety issue with one of its popular smartphone models, the TechGadget XPhone. The company has identified a potential battery overheating problem in a specific batch of phones. As a proactive measure, TechGadget Inc. has issued a voluntary recall of the affected devices and is asking customers to contact their customer service for a callback regarding the recall process.

Your Role as Participants:

You are customers who have purchased the TechGadget XPhone. You've received an email notification about the recall and have various concerns and questions regarding this issue. You will contact TechGadget Inc.'s customer service platform, which uses ChatGPT to handle initial inquiries and provide information.

Objectives:

Understand the recall process. Express your concerns or seek specific information about the product recall. Experience how AI can manage customer service inquiries in a real-world business scenario.
